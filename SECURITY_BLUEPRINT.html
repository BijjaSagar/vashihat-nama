The Security Blueprint

Building a digital vault that stays locked even if the server is compromised. This is Vasihat Nama.

🔐

Argon2id KDF

We use memory-hard hashing (Argon2id) to derive keys from your PIN. This makes GPU-based brute-force attacks effectively impossible, requiring massive memory resources that hackers cannot scale.

🧩

Shamir's Sharing

Keys are never stored in one piece. We use mathematical logical sharding to distribute trust, ensuring that a single point of failure cannot expose your vault.

🛰️

SSL Pinning

The app only talks to our verified server. We've pinned the SHA-256 certificate fingerprints, neutralizing "Man-in-the-Middle" (MITM) attacks even on compromised Wi-Fi networks.

👁️

ZK Discovery

Contact discovery is "blind." We store only cryptographically salted hashes of emails/phones. We know someone is a nominee, but we never know WHO they are until the trigger.

🛡️

Post-Quantum

Using AES-256-GCM for all data storage. GCM mode ensures both privacy AND authenticity—any attempt to modify your files while they are encrypted will be immediately detected.

🎭

Decoy Vault

Under duress, the app can surface a secondary "Decoy" session. It looks and acts exactly like the real app but contains only non-sensitive items, protecting you from physical coercion.

The Data Lifecycle: Efficient & Secret

How your data transforms from a readable document into an invisible, lightweight vault item.

🗜️

Stage 1: Adaptive Compression

Using Brotli (Web) or GZip (Mobile), we shrink your document. This removes repetitive data patterns, which both reduces S3 storage costs and prepares the data for high-entropy encryption.

💎

Stage 2: AES-256-GCM Encryption

The compressed stream is encrypted with an AES-256 cipher in Galois/Counter Mode (GCM). Unlike standard encryption, GCM provides Integrity—if a hacker modifies even one byte of the stored file, the decryption will fail instantly.

☁️

Stage 3: Blind Storage

The final output is a High-Entropy Ciphertext. To the cloud provider (AWS S3), it looks like random binary noise. There is no metadata, no file names, and no headers leaked. Your privacy is absolute.

Real-World Threat Scenarios

🌪️

Scenario: The Total Server Breach

The Attack: A hacker gains root access to our AWS S3 buckets and SQL Database.

The Result: They find millions of files, but they are all 100% encrypted. Without your local device-key and PIN, it would take 13.8 billion years to read even one single file. We don't have your keys, so the hacker can't steal them from us.

📡

Scenario: Public Wi-Fi Sniffing

The Attack: You use the app at an airport. A hacker sets up a "Man-in-the-Middle" attack to intercept your data.

The Result: Our SSL Certificate Pinning detects that the connection is not authentic. The app instantly kills the connection before a single byte of data is sent. The hacker sees nothing.

👊

Scenario: Physical Coercion

The Attack: Someone forces you to open the app and show them your "Vault."

The Result: You enter your Decoy PIN. The app opens a fully functional but "empty" vault. Your real sensitive data remains hidden in a separate encrypted partition that is mathematically invisible.
