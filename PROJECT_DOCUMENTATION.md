# Vasihat Nama - Project Documentation

## 1. Project Concept
**Vasihat Nama** (meaning "The Will") is an AI-powered legal and inheritance planning application. Its primary purpose is to solve the "lost assets" problem—where trillions of dollars globally are left in dormant bank accounts, digital wallets, and forgotten insurance policies because the owners passed away without sharing access with their families.

The app acts as a **Digital Vault** and a **Dead Man's Switch**. It allows users to store sensitive information (credentials, property titles, bank details) and automatically releases this access to "Trusted Nominees" only if the user fails to "Check-In" after a predefined period (Proof of Life).

## 2. Target Audience
- **Digital Asset Owners**: Individuals with crypto, digital wallets, and online accounts.
- **Expats & NRI**: People living abroad who need to manage regional legal compliance both at home and in their current country.
- **Senior Citizens**: Individuals looking for an easy, non-intimidating way to document their legacy.
- **Young Professionals**: Those who want to ensure their family is protected in case of unforeseen events.

## 4. Work Line Instructions (User Guide)

### Step 1: Secure Your Profile
Upon entry, the app uses **SMS OTP** for secure authentication. Go to the profile to ensure your mobile and email are correct.

### Step 2: Set Up Proof of Life
1. Navigate to the **Heartbeat Screen**.
2. Activate the **Proof of Life Protocol**.
3. Set your frequency (e.g., Check-in every 30 days).
*For testing, we've enabled a **"Fast Tracking"** mode where you can set this to just a few hours.*

### Step 3: Add Trusted Nominees
1. Go to **Trusted Nominees**.
2. Add a family member or friend.
3. Choose **Digital Mode** (Quick access) or **Physical Mode** (Requires address/ID for hardcopy handover).

### Step 4: Secure Your Documents
1. Open the **Secure Vault**.
2. Add passwords, notes, or upload sensitive documents.
3. **Crucial**: Tap on a document and select **"Assign Nominee"**. This binds that document to a specific person for the future.

### Step 5: Regional Compliance
1. Use the **Regional Compliance** screen.
2. Select your country (or type any country using the "Other" option).
3. Let the **AI Generate** a customized checklist of mandatory legal documents for that specific region.

## 5. Testing the "Proof of Life" (Heartbeat)
- To verify the system works without waiting months:
- Set frequency to **2 hours**.
- Do not log in or check-in.
- Trigger the Admin check (simulated).
- The Nominee will immediately see the "Access Granted" status in their view.
