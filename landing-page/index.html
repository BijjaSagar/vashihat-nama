The Process

How AfterSafe Works

A seamless, fully automated pipeline from secure storage to legacy transfer.

1

Encrypt & Store

You add your sensitive items (passwords, notes, crypto phrases, files). Everything is instantly encrypted on your device using AES-256 before ever reaching our servers. We cannot see your data. Period.

2

Assign Nominees

Add trusted family members, lawyers, or friends. You explicitly control exactly which folders or items each nominee gets. One gets the crypto, another gets the family photos.

3

Biometric Check-ins

You set a "Proof of Life" interval (e.g., every 30 days). The app notifies you to tap a button and scan your face/fingerprint. As long as you check in, the vault stays locked only for you.

4

Automated Handoff

If you fail to check in past your deadline, the dead man's switch triggers. The system automatically decrypts and securely emails/grants access to your chosen nominees.
